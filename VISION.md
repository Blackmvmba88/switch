# VISION: BlackMamba Cybernetic Runtime (BCR)

## Philosophy
The BlackMamba Cybernetic Runtime (BCR) is not just a controller mapper. It is a universal interoperability layer between human intent and machine execution. We believe that input is the most critical bottleneck in human-computer interaction, and our goal is to eliminate that bottleneck through semantic abstraction and high-fidelity translation.

## Objectives
- **Semantic Input:** Decouple physical hardware from logical intent.
- **Interoperability Universal:** Any device, any platform, one consistent semantic bus.
- **Cybernetic Runtime:** A low-latency, resilient execution environment that adapts to the user and the system.
- **Observability & Telemetry:** Full visibility into the input pipeline, from raw HID frames to browser injection.
- **Adaptive Control:** Future-proofing for AI-assisted gameplay and context-aware input mapping.

## The Future
We are moving towards a **Virtual HID** future where BCR lives as a core system service, providing an invisible, high-performance bridge for all gaming and professional input needs on macOS and beyond.
